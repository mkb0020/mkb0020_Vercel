02 · What CatForce Does

CatForce connects to third-party platforms including TikTok and X (formerly Twitter) via their official APIs to assist with content scheduling and publishing. Specifically:

  • It reads social media content you have submitted through the CatForce admin portal.
  • It uploads video content to your connected TikTok account's inbox as drafts for your review.
  • It assists with preparing text content for posting to X.
  • It logs posting history to a private database for scheduling purposes.

03 · Third-Party Platform Access

CatForce does not post publicly without your manual confirmation in the TikTok app. All uploads are sent as private drafts that you review and publish yourself.
